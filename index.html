VIZCAYA VIRTUAL EXPERIENCE

This project was made possible by the John S. and James L. Knight Foundation. Through the foundation's Museums and Technology Grant Vizcaya worked in collaboration with the University of Florida's Historic Preservation Program and Daruma Tech to enhance the visitor experience through 3D documenting and modelling technologies. These technologies enable visitors to explore and learn about spaces of Vizcaya that are not physically accessible to the public and enhance our preservation efforts.

Vizcaya recognizes its extreme vulnerability to sea level rise and climate change and is working to be a good model of resiliance for our community in the face of drastic and rapid change. We are contributing to the local and regional planning efforts that are in place and will be created. Our efforts with 3D documentation positions us as leaders on discussions related to preservation, climate change and sea level rise.
