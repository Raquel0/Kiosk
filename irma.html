Hurricane Irma

This model of the Barge shows some of the significant damage that resulted from Hurricane Irma in 2017. The elements that appear in red represent features that were damaged or swept into Biscayne Bay by the powerful storm surge and wind. The 3D documentation completed through this project continues to help staff explore ways to design for resiliency. Fragments from the Barge were salvaged in 2018 and after restoration, will be returned to their original locations. The conservation work on the Barge in particular is funded by the National Endowment for the Humanities.
